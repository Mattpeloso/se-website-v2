We provide an advanced metering database for stakeholders in the electricity industry. Users connect their meters to unlock the full potential of distributed generation, power networks, and interact with multiple like-minded users.

Our goal is to provide efficiencies in the power market by enabling every users role in the energy transition, making electricity more affordable and accessible to everyone.
