Control your data

Get insights on your energy production and consumption through our user portal

Use our publishing interface to share your data with the world. You can choose to make all your data public or restrict what you want to share with other users. Our platform allows you to control who can access your data and how it is used.

We provide a range of tools to help you visualize and analyze your data. You can create custom dashboards, set up alerts, and generate reports to gain insights into your energy usage and production.

For advanced users, we also provide additional services such as API access, data export and integration with third-party applications.

Let the world know about your contribution to a more sustainable world

Unleash the power of social media to communicate on your carbon reduction objectives. We offer a suite of tools that enables easy publication of your energy data on popular social media platforms.

Add your own brand to our publishing interface and share your achievements and inspire others to join the movement towards a greener future. Our platform makes it easy to create engaging content that highlights your commitment to sustainability.

Make difference within your community: Our energy allocation algorithm is tailored to prioritize local energy sharing, fostering a sense of community and collective effort towards sustainability.
